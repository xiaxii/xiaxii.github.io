Who am I

As shown in my linkedIn page, I am an undergraduate student in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications majoring in Internet of Things (IoT). The IoT program was established with Queen Mary University of London.
During the summer of 2019, I was an iSURE student in University of Notre Dame. And in Feburary 2020, I got admitted to the ESTEEM graduate program of Notre Dame.

What’s this blog for

Well, the blog is place for writing something. My interest of literature was initiated early since the primary school. And I kept gaining high marks in the Chinese courses. There was a time when I gave up writing after going to the colloge. Life became busy and all my study is about science. But I came to realize that writing is a good way to practice my ability to communicate and express. Also, blogging is a good way to record some technic skills during my study, such as the code and dev tools.
It took my some time to organize the blog. Now I got a plan for the structure:

  1. LeetCode I will record all the broblems that I’ve solved.
  2. Experience It includes some review of the turning points of my life.
  3. TED I am a fan of TED talks, I search for guide from those speakers every time I felt confused.
  4. Others are the projects that values to me, like 2019 Google NEXT - the recuiting activity of Google in 2019.

How is it going

Officially, this blog started in May 2019. But the idea was generated in 2018, and I tried some different ways to establish it.
In the future, I will keep updating in a regualar and orgnized way. Articles will have a clearer format.

Some tips for management

  1. 5W2H: What +Why +When +Where +Who +How +How much, suitable for innovation and planing.
  2. PDCA Cycle: Plan -> Do -> Check -> Act ->… suitable for Quality Maintaining.

Ways for expressing

  1. STAR: Situation + Task + Action + Result
