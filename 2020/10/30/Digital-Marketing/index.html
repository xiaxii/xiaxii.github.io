Introduction

How do new entrants in consumer product industry rise?

Digital technologies like digital cookies, content-curation websites (Reddit, BuzzFeed) and content-sharing websites (Slideshare, LinkedIn, Medium) lead to a new level of self-expression of individuals. Thus companies can better understand customers’ decision journey and subsequent word-of-mouth.

Digital Marketing landscape:

Paying for keywords

Golden Triangle - Upper left

  • Cost-Per-Click (CPC) bid: per keywords, position -> profitability -> Click Through Rate (CTR)
  • Quality Score: CTR, Relevence to consumers, Quality of the landing page

Assesing the Effectiveness

  1. CPC: CPC=Cost/Click
  2. CTR: CTR=Click/Impression(Impression: # of times seen by consumers)
  3. Convertion Rate: Convertion Rate=Applicationc Completed/Click
  4. Search Ad Profit = (impressions x click-through-rate x conversion rate x margin) - search ad cost
  5. ROI: ROI = Profit / Cost
